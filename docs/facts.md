## How it works with Discord / games (for "how-to" content)

The app takes your mic, applies effects, and outputs to a virtual audio cable
(VB-CABLE, installed automatically during setup). In Discord or your game, you
pick **"CABLE Output"** as your microphone — and everyone hears your new voice.
The in-app guided setup walks through it on first launch.

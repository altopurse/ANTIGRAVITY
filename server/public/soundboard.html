How the soundboard works

  • 1. Add sounds - drag & drop or upload MP3, WAV, OGG or M4A clips, record straight from your mic, or start with the built-in sample pack.
  • 2. Keybinds are automatic - each pad gets a key (1-9, then letters). Click ⌨ on a pad to rebind it to any key you like.
  • 3. Play - press the key or click the pad. Press again to restart (or tick "overlap" to layer sounds), Esc stops everything.
  • 4. Make it yours - rename pads (✎), set per-sound volume with the slider on each pad.
  • 5. Come back anytime - your board is saved in your browser, so your sounds and keybinds are still here tomorrow.

Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes - free, no signup, unlimited sounds. It's the web version of our free Windows soundboard app.

Are my sounds uploaded anywhere?

No. They're stored in your browser's local database on your own device.

Can I record my own sounds?

Yes - click ⏺ Record, allow the microphone, and your clip becomes a pad with its own keybind.

Do keybinds work while I'm gaming?

In the browser, only while this tab is focused. For global hotkeys that work in any game, grab the free Windows app.

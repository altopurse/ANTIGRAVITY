How the soundboard works

  • 1. Add sounds - upload MP3, WAV, OGG or M4A clips (memes, effects, music stings…).
  • 2. Keybinds are automatic - each pad gets a key (1-9, then letters). Click ⌨ on a pad to rebind it to any key you like.
  • 3. Play - press the key or click the pad. Press again to restart, Esc stops everything.
  • 4. Come back anytime - your board is saved in your browser, so your sounds and keybinds are still here tomorrow.
